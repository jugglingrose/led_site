ABOUT


LED Circus Show features SMART LED Hula Hoops, LED juggling, and a beautiful LED wings dance. The show can vary in length from 5-15 minutes in duration, depending on the clients needs. It can be performed as one continous show or broken up into 3 separate acts to be performed at different intervals during an event.


Customized LED acts and shows are available upon client request.


LED Circus Show is choreographed and performed by Corean Gonzales, a professional circus performer with several years of experience.
